Traffic road signs detection.
- scene color images containing traffic road signs with circular,
triangular or rectangular shape are given;
- you'll study and implement a method to detect all the traffic
road signs that appear in the images;
- the result consists in marking of all the detected traffic road
signs (or areas where they are located) over the original scene
images.
